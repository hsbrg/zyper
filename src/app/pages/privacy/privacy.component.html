Usage Information: We collect information about your interactions with our website, including IP addresses, browser type, language, access times, pages viewed, and the links you click.

Legal Compliance: We may disclose information about you if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with state and federal laws or respond to a court order, judicial or other government subpoena, or warrant.

Merger or Acquisition: In the event we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your personal information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

Cookies: We use cookies to collect information about your browsing behavior, preferences, and interactions with our website to enhance your user experience.

Google User Data Access

When you grant our application access to your Google account, we may access the following types of data:

  • Google Analytics data for website tracking and analysis
  • Google Ads account information for campaign management
  • Basic profile information (name and email) for account identification
  • Google Business Profile data for business listing management
  • Google Tag Manager data for website tracking and optimization

Data Usage and Storage: We only access and process this data to provide our marketing automation services. All Google user data is processed in accordance with Google's API Services User Data Policy and is not shared with third parties except as necessary to provide our services or as required by law.

Google Tag Manager and Analytics: Through Google Tag Manager and Analytics integration, we collect data about website usage patterns, including pages visited, time spent on site, and user interactions. You can opt-out of this tracking using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on or by adjusting your browser's cookie settings.

Data Retention and Account Control: We retain Google user data only for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You can revoke our access to your Google account data at any time through your Google Account settings or by contacting us directly. You may also request account deactivation by contacting our support team at contactus@zyper.ai.

Google Data Transfer Safety

When integrating with Google services, we ensure your data is transferred securely through:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transferred between our platform and Google's services is encrypted using industry-standard TLS (Transport Layer Security) protocols.
  • OAuth 2.0 Authentication: We use Google's secure OAuth 2.0 protocol for authentication, ensuring your Google account credentials are never directly handled by our systems.
  • API Security: All API calls to Google services are made using secure HTTPS connections and authenticated tokens, protecting your data during transmission.
  • Data Minimization: We only request and transfer the minimum necessary data required for the functionality you've authorized.
  • Regular Security Audits: We regularly review and update our Google integration security measures to maintain compliance with Google's security requirements.
